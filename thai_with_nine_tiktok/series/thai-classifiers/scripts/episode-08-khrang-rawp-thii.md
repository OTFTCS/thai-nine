Episode 08 - ครั้ง + รอบ + ที่

Target runtime: 70-100 seconds
Goal: teach abstract classifiers for events, cycles, and order — the twist that classifiers aren't just for physical objects

Full script:

[To camera]

**You've learned classifiers for things you can see and touch.**

**But Thai also counts things you can't hold.**

**ไปสองครั้ง** | bpai sǎawng khráng | go two TIMES | went two times
**[PERFORM] "Go two TIMES."**

**ดูหนังสองรอบ** | duu nǎng sǎawng râwp | watch movie two ROUNDS | watched the movie twice
**[PERFORM] "Watch movie two ROUNDS."**

**ครั้งที่สอง** | khráng thîi sǎawng | time PLACE two | the 2nd time
**[PERFORM] "Time PLACE two."**

**Three classifiers. All invisible.**

**ครั้ง -- say "krung" pushed UP high. รอบ -- say "rawp" with a falling pitch. ที่ -- say "tee" and drop it down.**

**ครั้ง counts times or occasions.**

**เคยไปเมืองไทยสามครั้ง** | khooei bpai meuuang-thai sǎam khráng | ever go Thailand three TIMES | been to Thailand three times
**[PERFORM] "Ever go Thailand three TIMES."**

**ครั้งนี้** | khráng níi | TIME this | this time
**[PERFORM] "TIME this." See? Classifiers work for pointing too — even at moments.**

**รอบ counts rounds or cycles.**

**ดูหนังสองรอบ** | duu nǎng sǎawng râwp | watch movie two ROUNDS | watched the movie twice
**[PERFORM] "Watch movie two ROUNDS."**

**รอบหน้า** | râwp nâa | ROUND next | next round
**[PERFORM] "ROUND next."**

**ที่ makes things ordinal — first, second, third.**

**ร้านที่สาม** | ráan thîi sǎam | shop PLACE three | the 3rd shop
**[PERFORM] "Shop PLACE three."**

**คนที่หนึ่ง** | khon thîi nùeng | person PLACE one | the 1st person
**[PERFORM] "Person PLACE one."**

**ครั้ง covers any occasion -- visits, attempts, meals out. รอบ is cycles, laps, showings. ที่ turns any number into 1st, 2nd, 3rd.**

[MINI-SCENE: Telling a friend about a trip]

**I've been to Thailand three times. The second time was the best.**

**เคยไปเมืองไทยสามครั้ง** | khooei bpai meuuang-thai sǎam khráng | ever go Thailand three TIMES
**ครั้งที่สองดีที่สุด** | khráng thîi sǎawng dii thîi sùt | time PLACE two good PLACE most | the 2nd time was the best
**[PERFORM] "Time PLACE two good PLACE most." Thai goes hard.**

How many times? ครั้ง.
Which lap or showing? รอบ.
What order? ที่.

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Quick check. "The first time"?**

[Reveal]
**ครั้งที่หนึ่ง** | khráng thîi nùeng | time PLACE one | the first time
**[PERFORM] "Time PLACE one."**

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Transfer check. "Watched it three rounds"?**

[Reveal]
**ดูสามรอบ** | duu sǎam râwp | watch three ROUNDS | watched it three times
**[PERFORM] "Watch three ROUNDS."**

**That's the full wave one. Eight videos. Fourteen classifiers. And the trick is always the same.**

[PERFORM, big text, montage callback from all episodes]
**"Dog BODY big very."**
**"Shirt three BODIES."**
**"Hammer ITEM big."**
**"Ticket FLAT-THING that."**
**"Phone MACHINE new."**
**"Orange ROUND-THING this."**
**"Coffee GLASS big."**
**"Time PLACE two."**

**Read the weird English. Remember the order. Sound Thai.**

**Comment "wave 2" if you want the next batch.**
