Thai Classifier Scripts

These are first-pass spoken scripts for the seven-video Thai classifier TikTok arc.

Each script is designed to:
- work as a standalone video
- stay under 3 minutes
- usually land in the 1-2 minute range
- open on contrast, not greeting
- keep the opener concept-first, not just counting-first
- keep one challenge beat and one transfer check
- format learner-facing Thai as `Thai | PTM transliteration | literal gloss | natural English`

These are human-authored recording scripts, not generator scaffolds.
