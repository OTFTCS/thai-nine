Episode 06 - ลูก vs ชิ้น

Target runtime: 70-100 seconds
Goal: make food and market classifiers instantly usable through performed glosses

Full script:

[Show a whole orange next to a slice of cake, both labeled "one ___"]

**One orange. One piece of cake. In English, both just "one."**

**In Thai?**

**ส้มหนึ่งลูก** | sôm nùeng lûuk | orange one ROUND-THING | one orange
**[PERFORM] "Orange one ROUND-THING."**

**เค้กหนึ่งชิ้น** | khéek nùeng chín | cake one PIECE | one piece of cake
**[PERFORM] "Cake one PIECE."**

**ลูก is for round or whole items. ชิ้น is for pieces, slices, portions.**

**ลูก -- say "look" but drop the pitch. Falling tone. ชิ้น -- say "chin" but push it UP high. High tone.**

**ทุเรียนสองลูก** | thú-riian sǎawng lûuk | durian two ROUND-THINGS | two durians
**[PERFORM] "Durian two ROUND-THINGS."**

**ลูกบอลหนึ่งลูก** | lûuk-baawn nùeng lûuk | ball one ROUND-THING | one ball
**[PERFORM] "Ball one ROUND-THING." And the word for ball literally starts with ลูก too.**

**พิซซ่าหนึ่งชิ้น** | phít-sâa nùeng chín | pizza one PIECE | one slice of pizza
**[PERFORM] "Pizza one PIECE."**

**ขนมปังหนึ่งชิ้น** | khà-nǒm-bpang nùeng chín | bread one PIECE | one piece of bread
**[PERFORM] "Bread one PIECE."**

**And for pointing and describing:**

**ส้มลูกนี้** | sôm lûuk níi | orange ROUND-THING this | this orange
**[PERFORM] "Orange ROUND-THING this."**

**เค้กชิ้นใหญ่** | khéek chín yài | cake PIECE big | big piece of cake
**[PERFORM] "Cake PIECE big."**

[MINI-SCENE: At a fruit stall]

**You're at a fruit stall. You want three durians.**

**ทุเรียนสามลูก** | thú-riian sǎam lûuk | durian three ROUND-THINGS | three durians
**[PERFORM] "Durian three ROUND-THINGS."**

**ลูก also covers balls, eggs, mountains, keys, and locks. ชิ้น works for any chunk -- sliced food, individual pieces, broken-off bits.**

Whole thing? ลูก.
Cut piece? ชิ้น.

**Last one in the series: same water, two completely different classifiers. How?**

**Comment a food noun and I'll tell you if it's ลูก or ชิ้น.**
