Episode 03 - อัน

Target runtime: 70-100 seconds
Goal: teach อัน as a useful fallback without letting it become the fake answer for everything

Full script:

[Rapid fire, buzzer energy on each]

**แมวหนึ่งอัน** | maaew nùeng an | cat one ITEM | one cat
[BUZZER] **Wrong.**

**นักเรียนหนึ่งอัน** | nák-riian nùeng an | student one ITEM | one student
[BUZZER] **Wrong.**

**พัดลมหนึ่งอัน** | phát-lom nùeng an | fan one ITEM | one fan
[BUZZER] **Still wrong.**

[To camera]

**If you use อัน for everything, Thai people will notice immediately.**

**But อัน does work — just for small, boring, general objects.**

**อัน -- say "un" like "under." Flat tone, dead simple.**

**รีโมตหนึ่งอัน** | rii-moht nùeng an | remote one ITEM | one remote
**[PERFORM] "Remote one ITEM." That works.**

**ไม้หนึ่งอัน** | mái nùeng an | stick one ITEM | one stick
**[PERFORM] "Stick one ITEM." That works.**

**ค้อนหนึ่งอัน** | kháawn nùeng an | hammer one ITEM | one hammer
**[PERFORM] "Hammer one ITEM." That works too.**

**And it works for pointing and describing too.**

**รีโมตอันนี้** | rii-moht an níi | remote ITEM this | this remote
**[PERFORM] "Remote ITEM this."**

**ค้อนอันใหญ่** | kháawn an yài | hammer ITEM big | big hammer
**[PERFORM] "Hammer ITEM big."**

[Beat]

**So what went wrong with the first three?**

**They already have their own classifiers.**

**แมวหนึ่งตัว** | maaew nùeng dtua | cat one BODY | one cat
**[PERFORM] "Cat one BODY" — not ITEM.**

**นักเรียนหนึ่งคน** | nák-riian nùeng khon | student one PERSON | one student
**[PERFORM] "Student one PERSON" — not ITEM.**

**พัดลมหนึ่งเครื่อง** | phát-lom nùeng khrêuang | fan one MACHINE | one fan
**[PERFORM] "Fan one MACHINE" — not ITEM.**

**อัน is the fallback, not the cheat code.**

**อัน covers things like remotes, tools, cushions, small gadgets, toys -- anything small without its own classifier.**

Small and generic? อัน.
Has its own classifier? Use that instead.

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Quick check. Which sounds right?**
**แมวหนึ่งอัน or แมวหนึ่งตัว?**

[Reveal]
**แมวหนึ่งตัว** | maaew nùeng dtua | cat one BODY | one cat
**[PERFORM] "Cat one BODY." Always.**

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Transfer check. "One magazine" — อัน or something else?**

[Reveal]
**นิตยสารหนึ่งเล่ม** | nít-dtà-yaa-sǎan nùeng lèm | magazine one VOLUME | one magazine
**[PERFORM] "Magazine one VOLUME." Not ITEM.**

**Next: a ticket and a book look nothing alike, so why would Thai count them the same way?**

**Stitch this with a noun and I'll tell you if อัน works or not.**
