Episode 06 - ลูก vs ชิ้น

Target runtime: 70-90 seconds
Goal: make food and market classifiers instantly usable

Full script:

One orange and one piece of cake count differently.

Use "ลูก" for round or whole items.

ส้มหนึ่งลูก | sôm nùeng lûuk | orange one round-thing | one orange

มะม่วงสองลูก | má-mûang sǎawng lûuk | mango two round-things | two mangoes

ลูกบอลหนึ่งลูก | lûuk-baawn nùeng lûuk | ball one round-thing | one ball

Use "ชิ้น" for pieces, slices, or portions.

เค้กหนึ่งชิ้น | khéek nùeng chín | cake one piece | one piece of cake

พิซซ่าหนึ่งชิ้น | phít-sâa nùeng chín | pizza one piece | one slice of pizza

ขนมปังหนึ่งชิ้น | khà-nǒm-bpang nùeng chín | bread one piece | one piece of bread

Fast shortcut.

Whole object, think "ลูก".

Cut piece, think "ชิ้น".

Orange one round-thing.

Cake one piece.

Quick check.

แตงโมหนึ่งลูก | dtaaeng-mooh nùeng lûuk | watermelon one round-thing | one watermelon

Transfer check.

ปลาแซลมอนหนึ่งชิ้น | bplaa-saaen-maawn nùeng chín | salmon one piece | one piece of salmon

Comment a food noun and I will tell you if it is "ลูก" or "ชิ้น".

On-screen beats:
- Open on a whole orange next to a cake slice
- Group the "ลูก" examples visually, then switch to the "ชิ้น" examples
- End on the salmon transfer check with a short answer pause:
  ปลาแซลมอนหนึ่งชิ้น | bplaa-saaen-maawn nùeng chín | salmon one piece | one piece of salmon
