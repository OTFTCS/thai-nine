Episode 10 — ทุก (thúk): "every"

Target runtime: 70–90 seconds
Goal: show that ทุก slots in front of classifiers and time words to unlock "everyone / everywhere / everything / every time" — a payoff for the whole classifier series

Hook angle: ทุก is one syllable. It sounds like you're done before you've started. But drop it in front of a classifier and you have a whole category covered.

Full script:

[Flash three phrases rapid-fire with no context, stacked on screen]

ทุกวัน
ทุกคน
ทุกที่

[To camera. Beat.]

**One word hides in all three of those. And once you see it, you can't unsee it.**

**ทุก** | thúk | every
**[PERFORM] "Every." One syllable. Rising tone. Say thúk — the accent mark tells you to flick the pitch up.**

**Drop it in front of a word and it means every one of that thing.**

**ทุกวัน** | thúk wan | every day | every day
**[PERFORM] "Every day."**

**ทุกคน** | thúk khon | every PERSON | everyone
**[PERFORM] "Every PERSON." คน — that's the people classifier. You know that one.**

**ทุกตัว** | thúk dtua | every BODY | every single one (animals, clothing, objects)
**[PERFORM] "Every BODY."**

**ทุก doesn't care what you pair it with. It just means "every" of that category.**

**ทุกอย่าง** | thúk yàang | every TYPE | everything
**[PERFORM] "Every TYPE." อย่าง is the classifier for types or kinds — so "every type" becomes "everything."**

**ทุกครั้ง** | thúk khráng | every TIME | every time
**[PERFORM] "Every TIME."**

**ทุกที่** | thúk thîi | every PLACE | everywhere
**[PERFORM] "Every PLACE."**

**Six phrases. One word. ทุก.**

[MINI-SCENE: friend asking about a habit]

**Friend: Do you eat Thai food often?**

**ทุกวันกินข้าว** | thúk wan gin khâao | every day eat rice | I eat rice every day
**[PERFORM] "Every day eat rice."**

**ทุกคนชอบผัดไทย** | thúk khon châwp phàt-thai | every PERSON like Pad Thai | everyone loves Pad Thai
**[PERFORM] "Every PERSON like Pad Thai."**

**Thai people will absolutely nod at that one.**

ทุก + day = ทุกวัน.
ทุก + classifier = every one of that category.

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Quick check. "Every time"?**

[Reveal]
**ทุกครั้ง** | thúk khráng | every TIME | every time
**[PERFORM] "Every TIME."**

[PAUSE 3s — "YOUR TURN" on screen]

**Transfer check. "Everywhere"?**

[Reveal]
**ทุกที่** | thúk thîi | every PLACE | everywhere
**[PERFORM] "Every PLACE."**

**One word. Six phrases unlocked. That's ทุก.**

**Comment ทุกวัน if you're going to use this today.**
