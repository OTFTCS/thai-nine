Episode 05 - คัน vs เครื่อง

Target runtime: 70-100 seconds
Goal: lock in vehicles vs machines through contrast and performed glosses

Full script:

[Cut between a car and a phone, Thai text on screen, no explanation yet]

**รถหนึ่งคัน** | rót nùeng khan | car one VEHICLE | one car
**โทรศัพท์หนึ่งเครื่อง** | thoo-rá-sàp nùeng khrêuang | phone one MACHINE | one phone

[To camera]

**Thai counts these completely differently.**

**[PERFORM] "Car one VEHICLE."**
**[PERFORM] "Phone one MACHINE."**

**คัน is for anything that moves you around. เครื่อง is for machines and devices.**

**คัน -- say "kun" with a flat tone. เครื่อง -- say "kreuang" and drop the pitch. Falling tone, and it's a mouthful.**

**จักรยานหนึ่งคัน** | jàk-gra-yaan nùeng khan | bicycle one VEHICLE | one bicycle
**[PERFORM] "Bicycle one VEHICLE."**

**มอเตอร์ไซค์หนึ่งคัน** | maaw-dtêr-sai nùeng khan | motorbike one VEHICLE | one motorbike
**[PERFORM] "Motorbike one VEHICLE."**

**คอมพิวเตอร์หนึ่งเครื่อง** | khaawm-phiw-dtôoe nùeng khrêuang | computer one MACHINE | one computer
**[PERFORM] "Computer one MACHINE."**

**ตู้เย็นหนึ่งเครื่อง** | dtûu-yen nùeng khrêuang | refrigerator one MACHINE | one refrigerator
**[PERFORM] "Refrigerator one MACHINE." Your fridge is a machine, apparently.**

**Same pattern for pointing and describing.**

**รถตุ๊กตุ๊กคันนี้** | rót dtúk-dtúk khan níi | tuk-tuk VEHICLE this | this tuk-tuk
**[PERFORM] "Tuk-tuk VEHICLE this."**

**คอมพิวเตอร์เครื่องใหม่** | khaawm-phiw-dtôoe khrêuang mài | computer MACHINE new | new computer
**[PERFORM] "Computer MACHINE new."**

[MINI-SCENE: Pointing at motorbikes on a Thai street]

**You're walking down the street. You point at a motorbike.**

**มอเตอร์ไซค์คันนั้น** | maaw-dtêr-sai khan nán | motorbike VEHICLE that | that motorbike
**[PERFORM] "Motorbike VEHICLE that."**

**คัน also covers umbrellas and fishing rods -- anything with a handle you steer. เครื่อง covers fridges, washing machines, air conditioners, engines.**

If it moves you? คัน.
If it's a device? เครื่อง.
