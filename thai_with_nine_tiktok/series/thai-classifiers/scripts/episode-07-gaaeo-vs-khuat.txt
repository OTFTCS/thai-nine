Episode 07 - แก้ว vs ขวด

Target runtime: 70-90 seconds
Goal: finish the arc with a practical cafe and shop use case

Full script:

Same water, different classifier.

Use "แก้ว" for a glass or cup serving.

น้ำหนึ่งแก้ว | náam nùeng gâaeo | water one glass | one glass of water

กาแฟหนึ่งแก้ว | gaa-faae nùeng gâaeo | coffee one glass | one coffee

ชาไทยหนึ่งแก้ว | chaa-thai nùeng gâaeo | Thai-tea one glass | one Thai tea

Use "ขวด" for a bottle.

น้ำหนึ่งขวด | náam nùeng khùat | water one bottle | one bottle of water

โค้กหนึ่งขวด | khóok nùeng khùat | Coke one bottle | one bottle of Coke

น้ำผลไม้หนึ่งขวด | náam phǒn-lá-mái nùeng khùat | fruit-juice one bottle | one bottle of juice

So the noun can stay the same.

The container changes, so the classifier changes.

Water one glass.

Water one bottle.

Quick check.

กาแฟเย็นหนึ่งแก้ว | gaa-faae yen nùeng gâaeo | iced-coffee one glass | one iced coffee

Transfer check.

นมหนึ่งขวด | nom nùeng khùat | milk one bottle | one bottle of milk

If you want wave two classifiers, comment "part 2".
