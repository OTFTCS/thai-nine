Episode 02 - คน vs ตัว

Target runtime: 70-100 seconds
Goal: make the people-vs-animal split feel automatic

Full script:

Why is one cat not counted like one person in Thai?

Because Thai splits people and animals.

Use "คน" for people.

นักเรียนหนึ่งคน | nák-riian nùeng khon | student one person | one student

หมอสองคน | mǎaw sǎawng khon | doctor two people | two doctors

Use "ตัว" for animals and body-shaped things.

ปลาหนึ่งตัว | bplaa nùeng dtua | fish one body | one fish

นกสองตัว | nók sǎawng dtua | bird two bodies | two birds

ตุ๊กตาหนึ่งตัว | dtúk-dtaa nùeng dtua | doll one body | one doll

Fast shortcut.

Human, think "คน".

Animal or body-shaped thing, think "ตัว".

If the strange English sounds weird, good.

That is the memory trick.

Quick check.

ม้าหนึ่งตัว | máa nùeng dtua | horse one body | one horse

Transfer check.

เพื่อนหนึ่งคน | phêuuan nùeng khon | friend one person | one friend

Comment a noun and I will tell you if it takes "คน" or "ตัว".

On-screen beats:
- Open on one student and one cat in fast succession
- Hold the two classifier labels side by side: "คน" and "ตัว"
- End on the friend transfer check as a pause card:
  เพื่อนหนึ่งคน | phêuuan nùeng khon | friend one person | one friend
