# Privacy Notice — Thai with Nine

**Effective date:** {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}

This page explains what happens to your information when you sign up for a free Thai PDF or weekly emails from Thai with Nine. We've written it in plain English, not legal jargon.

## Who we are

Thai with Nine is a small independent Thai-language teaching project run by Nine and Oliver. We make free Thai PDFs and (soon) a structured Thai course called Immersion Thai. You can find us on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube as **@thaiwith.nine**.

## What we collect

When you sign up for a free PDF, we collect:

- Your **email address**
- Your **first name** (optional — you can leave it blank)
- The **lead magnet you signed up for** (e.g. "Thai Classifiers Cheat Sheet")
- The **date and time** you signed up

That's it. We don't ask for or collect anything else — no addresses, no phone numbers, no payment information, no tracking cookies beyond what's strictly necessary to make the signup form work.

## Why we collect it

We use your email and first name to:

1. **Send you the free Thai PDF** you requested.
2. **Send you a short welcome series of 5 emails** over your first 17 days, helping you get the most out of the PDF and learn a bit more Thai.
3. **Send you a new free Thai PDF every Monday**, with the option to unsubscribe at any time.
4. **Let you know when the Immersion Thai course launches** — only if you've explicitly joined the waitlist.

We do not use your email for anything else. We do not run targeted ads. We do not profile you.

## Where your data is stored

All subscriber data is stored in **MailerLite**, an email service provider based in the EU (Lithuania). MailerLite is GDPR-compliant and acts as our data processor. You can read MailerLite's own privacy policy at [mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy).

We do not store your email anywhere else. We do not export it to spreadsheets, CRMs, or other tools.

## Who we share it with

**Nobody.** We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your email address with anyone. Ever. Not with marketing partners, not with affiliates, not with other teachers. Your email stays between you, us, and MailerLite.

## How long we keep it

We keep your subscriber record for as long as you stay subscribed. If you unsubscribe, MailerLite removes you from the active list within a few minutes and deletes your record from our active subscribers within 30 days.

If you want us to delete your data immediately, see "Your rights" below.

## Your rights

Under GDPR (and similar laws in the UK, US, and elsewhere), you have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you
- **Correct** anything that's wrong
- **Delete** your data entirely ("right to be forgotten")
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (just unsubscribe — link at the bottom of every email)
- **Object** to how we're processing your data
- **Complain** to your local data protection authority if you think we're doing something wrong

To exercise any of these rights, just email **{{CONTACT_EMAIL}}** with what you want and we'll handle it within 7 days.

You can also unsubscribe instantly by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send you.

## Cookies and tracking

The MailerLite-hosted signup pages and emails use minimal cookies — only the essential ones needed to make forms work and track email opens / clicks (so we know if our content is useful). We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking tools.

## Children

Thai with Nine is intended for adults learning Thai. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has signed up, please email us at {{CONTACT_EMAIL}} and we'll delete the record immediately.

## Changes to this notice

If we ever change how we handle data, we'll update this page and email anyone on the list to let them know. The most recent effective date will always be at the top.

## Contact

For anything privacy-related: **{{CONTACT_EMAIL}}**

For everything else (Thai questions, course questions, feedback): same email or DM us on TikTok at **@thaiwith.nine**.
