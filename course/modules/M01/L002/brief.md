# M01-L002 — The Thai Sound System

- **Primary objective:** Distinguish consonants, short/long vowels, and 5 tones at a beginner level.
- **Secondary objective:** Hear near-minimal contrasts in common words.
- **Scenario:** Learner listens to Thai words that sound similar but differ by tone/vowel length.

## Key vocabulary
- ไก่ | gai | chicken
- ไข่ | khai | egg
- ใกล้ | glai (falling) | near
- ไกล | glai (mid) | far
