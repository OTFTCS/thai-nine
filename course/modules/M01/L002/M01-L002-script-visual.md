# Visual Script — M01-L002 (Names and Countries)

## Frame rule
- Keep all teaching visuals in the left two-thirds.
- The right third stays clear for Nine's talking head.
- Prioritise sentence frames and ladders over decorative identity imagery.

## Section 1 — Ask and answer the name question
- Layout: dialogue-ladder
- Learning focus: fixed question-answer pair
- Highlighted terms:
  - ชื่อ | chûue | name
  - คุณชื่ออะไร | khun chûue à-rai | what is your name?
  - ฉันชื่อ... | chǎn chûue... | my name is ...
  - ผมชื่อ... | phǒm chûue... | my name is ... (male speaker)
- On-screen plan:
  - one question line
  - one answer line
  - highlighted name slot
- Visual support choice: text-only

## Section 2 — Use ฉัน and ผม naturally
- Layout: contrast-board
- Learning focus: practical pronoun choice
- Highlighted terms:
  - ฉัน | chǎn | I
  - ผม | phǒm | I (male)
  - ฉันชื่อ... | chǎn chûue... | my name is ...
  - ผมชื่อ... | phǒm chûue... | my name is ... (male speaker)
- On-screen plan:
  - speaker card contrast
  - one small note that คน means person
  - one full introduction line under the cards
- Visual support choice: text-only

## Section 3 — Say where you are from with มาจาก
- Layout: image-anchored
- Learning focus: origin pattern plus country category
- Highlighted terms:
  - ประเทศ | bprà-thêet | country
  - มาจาก | maa-jàak | come from / be from
  - มาจากไทย | maa-jàak thai | be from Thailand
  - มาจากอังกฤษ | maa-jàak ang-grìt | be from England
  - มาจากอเมริกา | maa-jàak à-mee-rí-gaa | be from America
- On-screen plan:
  - ประเทศ category chip at the top
  - sentence frame card dominates the left panel
  - small flag or map cues under the country words
- Real-world image helpful: yes, but only lightly
- Search ideas:
  - simple Thailand England America flags flat lay
  - country flag icons Thailand England America

## Section 4 — Build a full two-line self-introduction
- Layout: dialogue-ladder
- Learning focus: stack greeting, name, country, and review cues
- Highlighted terms:
  - สวัสดีครับ/ค่ะ | sà-wàt-dii khráp/khâ | hello
  - ขอโทษ | khǎaw-thôot | excuse me / sorry
  - ฉันชื่อ... / ผมชื่อ... | chǎn/phǒm chûue... | my name is ...
  - ฉันมาจาก... / ผมมาจาก... | chǎn/phǒm maa-jàak... | I am from ...
  - ใช่ | châi | yes / that is right
  - ขอบคุณ | khàawp-khun | thank you
- On-screen plan:
  - four-step introduction ladder
  - one small note that คน means person in the conversation
  - small confirmation chip for ใช่
  - small closing thank-you chip
- Visual support choice: text-only

## Roleplay beat
- Show one turn at a time on the left side.
- Keep the question, country answer, and confirmation turns clearly separated.
- Let อังกฤษใช่ไหมคะ and ใช่ครับ appear as a tight confirmation pair.
- Do not let the country visual anchor expand into the right-third camera zone.
