# M01-L005 — Where and What Questions

- **Module:** M01 — First Contact and Courtesy
- **Level:** A0
- **Target runtime:** 10-13 minutes
- **Primary objective:** Learner can ask what something is and where something is in short practical Thai.
- **Secondary objective:** Learner can recycle recent vocabulary while using 'ที่ไหน' with short controlled answers such as 'อยู่ที่นี่' and 'อยู่ที่นั่น'.
- **Teaching frame:** A new learner is at a small school or community centre, sees a map, and needs to ask what an object is and where to go.

## Must-teach outcomes
1. Recognise and use the question word 'อะไร' in fixed beginner chunks such as 'นี่อะไร'.
2. Recognise and use the where-question 'ที่ไหน' with the verb 'อยู่'.
3. Understand the core pointing words 'นี่', 'นั่น', 'ที่นี่', and 'ที่นั่น'.
4. Ask about a real place with 'ห้องน้ำอยู่ที่ไหน'.
5. Reuse recent review material such as 'คุณ', 'เขา', and the digits 'ศูนย์' to 'สาม' inside short controlled output.

## Core vocabulary and chunks
| Thai | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| อะไร | à-rai | what |
| ที่ไหน | thîi-nǎi | where |
| อยู่ | yùu | be located / live |
| ที่นี่ | thîi-nîi | here |
| ที่นั่น | thîi-nân | there |
| นี่ | nîi | this |
| นั่น | nân | that |
| นี่อะไร | nîi à-rai | what is this? |
| คุณอยู่ที่ไหน | khun yùu thîi-nǎi | where do you live / where are you? |
| ห้องน้ำอยู่ที่ไหน | hâawng-náam yùu thîi-nǎi | where is the bathroom? |
| อยู่ที่นี่ | yùu thîi-nîi | be here |
| อยู่ที่นั่น | yùu thîi-nân | be there |

## Teaching constraints
- Keep the lesson as a vocabulary-and-question system lesson, not a grab-bag of new nouns.
- Explain that Thai often leaves the question word at the end of the chunk.
- Explain the job of 'อยู่' simply: with places it means be located; with people it can mean live or be/stay.
- Reuse review vocabulary naturally, especially 'คุณ', 'เขา', and the digits 'ศูนย์' to 'สาม'.
- Keep visuals in the left two-thirds of the frame.

## Likely learner mistakes
1. Translating 'นี่อะไร' word by word instead of learning it as a useful whole chunk.
2. Forgetting that Thai often puts the question word near the end of the sentence.
3. Treating 'อยู่' as only one English verb and missing its practical location job.
4. Freezing when a location answer is short, such as 'อยู่ที่นี่' or 'อยู่ที่นั่น'.
5. Learning 'ที่ไหน' without plugging it into a real place question.

## Image and visual notes
- Sections 1 and 3 should stay text-first.
- Section 2 benefits from one real-world wayfinding or bathroom-sign image so the location question feels concrete.
- Section 4 can use a small map or reception-desk visual anchor, but keep the text dominant.
