# Visual Script — M01-L003 (Pronouns and Who Questions)

## Frame rule
- Keep all teaching visuals in the left two-thirds.
- The right third stays clear for Nine's talking head.
- Prioritise identity frames and who-questions over decorative imagery.

## Section 1 — Use pronouns as practical speaker labels
- Layout: contrast-board
- Learning focus: self versus another person
- Highlighted terms:
  - ฉัน | chǎn | I
  - ผม | phǒm | I (male speaker)
  - เขา | khǎo | he / she
  - คุณชื่ออะไร | khun chûue à-rai | what is your name?
- On-screen plan:
  - three speaker chips
  - one review strip for คุณชื่ออะไร
- Visual support choice: text-only

## Section 2 — Use นี่คือ... to introduce and identify
- Layout: dialogue-ladder
- Learning focus: introduction frame with คือ
- Highlighted terms:
  - นี่คือ... | nîi kheuu... | this is ...
  - เขาคือ... | khǎo kheuu... | he / she is ...
  - คุณคือ...ใช่ไหม | khun kheuu... châi mái | you are ... right?
  - ไม่ใช่ | mâi châi | no / not right
- On-screen plan:
  - three-rung identity ladder
  - small yes/no response chip
- Real-world image helpful: yes, but only lightly
- Search ideas:
  - two people introducing a third person in classroom
  - small group introduction language class

## Section 3 — Ask who someone is with ใคร
- Layout: image-anchored
- Learning focus: identity question versus name question
- Highlighted terms:
  - เขาคือใคร | khǎo kheuu khrai | who is he / she?
  - คนนี้คือใคร | khon níi kheuu khrai | who is this person?
  - ชื่อ | chûue | name
  - ใช่ | châi | yes / that is right
- On-screen plan:
  - person card with the question above it
  - contrast strip below for คุณชื่ออะไร versus เขาคือใคร
- Real-world image helpful: yes
- Search ideas:
  - single person portrait simple background looking at camera
  - student portrait ID style simple background

## Section 4 — Build a mini identity exchange with review
- Layout: drill-stack
- Learning focus: greeting to who-question to country follow-up
- Highlighted terms:
  - ขอโทษนะ | khǎaw-thôot ná | excuse me
  - คนนี้คือใคร | khon níi kheuu khrai | who is this person?
  - ประเทศ | bprà-thêet | country
  - เขามาจากไทย | khǎo maa-jàak thai | he / she is from Thailand
  - คุณคือ...ใช่ไหม | khun kheuu... châi mái | you are ... right?
- On-screen plan:
  - four-step conversation stack
  - small country tag chip
  - reveal one line at a time
- Visual support choice: text-only

## Roleplay beat
- Show one turn at a time on the left side.
- Keep the who-question and country question visually separate.
- Let อังกฤษใช่ไหมคะ and ใช่ครับ appear as a tight confirmation pair.
- Do not let the person-image anchor crowd the right-third camera zone.
