# Visual Script — M01-L003: Pronouns and Who Questions

## Layout
16:9 slides. Top-right corner reserved for Nine's camera (PiP). All teaching content in the safe zone.

## Slide plan

### Slide 1 — Opener
- Eyebrow: M01-L003
- Title: "Pronouns and Who Questions"
- Subtitle: You can now use I, you, he/she and ask who someone is.
- Branding: Immersion Thai with Nine

### Slide 2 — Objectives
- What you will learn today:
  - Say I, you, he/she in Thai
  - Build "this is..." sentences
  - Ask "who is that?"
  - Confirm someone's identity

### Slide 3 — Section 1: I and you
- Three focus cards: ฉัน / ผม / คุณ
- Each with transliteration and English on separate lines
- Gender note on ผม (male, polite)
- Practice drills below cards

### Slide 4 — Section 2: He, she, and this
- Three word cards: เขา / นี่ / คือ
- One chunk card: นี่คือ...
- Practice drills below

### Slide 5 — Section 3: Who questions
- Question word card: ใคร
- Two chunk cards: เขาคือใคร / คุณคือ...ใช่ไหม
- Practice drills below

### Slide 6 — Section 4: Mini exchange
- Dialogue ladder showing the identity exchange
- 3 review vocab items recycled in context

### Slide 7 — Pronunciation beat
- Minimal pair: ใคร (rising) contrast
- Tone echo drill

### Slide 8 — Roleplay
- 6-line dialogue: You + Friend
- Speaker pills with three-line display (Thai / translit / English)

### Slide 9 — Recap
- 5 retrieval prompts
- Key takeaway
