## Immersion Tips

Three quick challenges before you close the lesson.

First: next time you see a photo of your own family or a friend's, label the people out loud in Thai — สามี, ภรรยา, ลูกชาย, ลูกสาว. Make it a small habit.

Second: open your course flashcard deck and drill today's seven family words. Pay special attention to ปู่ and ย่า — say them out loud with the correct tone, not just read them silently.

Third: search the hashtag ครอบครัว on Instagram or Facebook. Thai people post family photos constantly and you'll see all of today's words used in real captions. That's input flood in the wild.

See you in the next lesson.
