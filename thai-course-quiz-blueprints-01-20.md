# Thai Course — Quiz Blueprints: Lessons 01–20

Per-lesson quizzes: 5–8 questions each. Mix of recognition + production.
Question types: Audio meaning match (AMM), Thai→English (T→E), English→Thai (E→T), Word order arrangement (WO), Real-world response choice (RW).
