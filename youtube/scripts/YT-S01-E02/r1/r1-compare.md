## PART 3 first vocab item example sentence

Vocab item v-001 (อยู่ที่ไหน). Same vocab across variants, different example sentence quality.

### Variant r1-A
- **ร้านกาแฟอยู่ที่ไหนคะ** — *ráan gaa-fae yùu thîi nǎi khá*
- *Translation:* Where is the coffee shop?
- *Note:* Same pattern for any place — just say the place, then อยู่ที่ไหน at the end. คะ is the polite particle for women.

### Variant r1-B
- **ร้านกาแฟอยู่ที่ไหนคะ** — *ráan gaa-fae yùu thîi nǎi khá*
- *Translation:* Where is the coffee shop?
- *Note:* Same pattern for any place — just say the place, then อยู่ที่ไหน at the end. คะ is the polite particle for women.

### Variant r1-C
- **ร้านกาแฟอยู่ที่ไหนคะ** — *ráan gaa-fae yùu thîi nǎi khá*
- *Translation:* Where is the coffee shop?
- *Note:* Same pattern for any place — just say the place, then อยู่ที่ไหน at the end. คะ is the polite particle for women.

### Variant r1-D
- **ขอโทษค่ะ พอดีหาห้องน้ำอยู่ ห้องน้ำอยู่ที่ไหนคะ** — *khǎaw-thôot khâ phaaw-dii hǎa hâwng náam yùu hâwng náam yùu thîi nǎi khá*
- *Translation:* Excuse me, I happen to be looking for the bathroom -- where is the bathroom?
- *Note:* พอดี... อยู่ is the move that signals 'I'm in the middle of looking for X' and softens the question. Without it the question can sound abrupt to a stranger. Thai people use it to telegraph that they're not just demanding information, they're already trying.

## PART 7 drill 1 -- prompt + answer

### Variant r1-A

**Prompt block:**
- How would you ask: 'Where is the bathroom?'
- Try saying it now...

**Answer block:**
- **ห้องน้ำอยู่ที่ไหนคะ** — *hâwng náam yùu thîi nǎi khá*
- That's right — ห้องน้ำอยู่ที่ไหน means 'where is the bathroom?'
