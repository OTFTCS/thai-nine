## EXPLAIN

> *Pick ONE cultural fact to open with. Option A (default): Street food scale — 14,000 vendors, sharing culture. Option B: Tom Yum Kung UNESCO 2024, fifth heritage item. Option C: Thai meals are shared — nobody orders one dish, small spoonfuls from shared plates. Delete the unused options after recording.*

Bangkok has over 14,000 registered street food vendors. It was named the world's number one street food city. And Thai food culture is built on sharing — you never order just one dish for yourself. Everyone gets rice, and you take small spoonfuls from shared plates. So the vendor just needs to know what you want. And that starts with one phrase:
สั่งอะไรดีคะ
By the end of this video, you won't need to point. You'll order in Thai, ask for recommendations, tell them how spicy you want it, and get the bill — all in Thai.
