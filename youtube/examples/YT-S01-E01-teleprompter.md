# YT-S01-E01 — Ordering Food in Thai — Your First 8 Phrases

---

## HOOK

> *Energetic, look to camera. Say the Thai phrase then pause 1 beat.*

Today you'll learn 8 phrases to order food in Thai like a local.

---

## EXPLAIN

> *Warm, personal. Share your own experience at the noodle stall. Make the viewer feel like they're there with you.*

When I first moved to Bangkok, there was this noodle stall near my apartment. Every morning, the same aunty would shout at me — สั่งอะไรดีคะ — and I had no idea what to say. I just pointed at the picture.
สั่งอะไรดีคะ
By the end of this video, you won't need to point. You'll order in Thai, ask for recommendations, tell them how spicy you want it, and get the bill — all in Thai.
