# YT-S01-E03 — Taking a Taxi in Thai — Tell the Driver Where to Go

## EXPLAIN

> *Conversational, personal. Tell your story about the Bangkok taxi scam. Laugh about it — it's a rite of passage.*

OK, story time. The first taxi I ever took in Bangkok, the driver looked at me and said 'two hundred baht'. I had no idea if that was a lot or a little — so I said yes. Turns out the meter would have been sixty baht. I paid three times the price because I didn't know one phrase.
เปิดมิเตอร์ด้วยค่ะ
That phrase right there would have saved me 140 baht. Let's make sure it doesn't happen to you.
