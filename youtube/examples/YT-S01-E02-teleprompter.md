## EXPLAIN

> *Warm, personal. Tell your real story about getting lost in Bangkok. Make it relatable and funny.*

My first week in Bangkok, I got completely lost trying to find a BTS station. The signs were all in Thai, my phone was dead, and I was sweating in 35-degree heat. I stopped an old lady and just said 'BTS?' with a confused face. She laughed, grabbed my arm, and walked me there herself. Thai people are incredibly kind — but it's so much better when you can actually ask properly.

By the end of this video, you'll know how to ask where anything is — and understand directions like 'go straight' and 'turn left'.
